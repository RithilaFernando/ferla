About us

OUR MISSION

To train individuals to master the perfect fit — because fashion doesn’t turn heads until it fits right. We focus on construction that complements the human form in 3D, ensuring quality and client satisfaction every time.

OUR VISION

To be Sri Lanka’s leading center for practical fashion education, producing professionals who blend creativity with precision and set new standards in garment making.

My Experience

With over 35 years in the fashion clothing industry, I’ve had the privilege of training thousands of students — from beginners to professionals. My journey began with a passion for garment construction and evolved into a mission: to raise the standards of fit and finish in the Sri Lankan fashion scene. As CEO of Ferla Apparel Tech, I’ve worked closely with manufacturers, designers, and clients, always emphasizing the importance of quality and custom fit.

My teaching focuses not just on theory, but on practical skill — helping each student understand how to shape fabric around the human body in a way that’s both functional and beautiful. I take personal responsibility in every student’s development, ensuring they leave with the confidence and ability to face the fashion industry's challenges head-on.
